Model 591

Description:

Thermacore® is the premier insulated door available today. It is an ideal choice for applications
that demand the highest levels of thermal efficiency, air infilteration, and windload resistance.
The 591 Series is one of the best-selling doors in the Thermacore® line, and offers superior thermal efficiency
to fit a broad range of applications. The 591 Series exterior skin is constructed of galvanized steel with a ribbed,
textured finish. It is available with a white interior finish and either a white or industrial brown exterior.
